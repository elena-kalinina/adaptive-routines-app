# Demo Scenario (~2.5 minutes)

## Story Arc

**Act 1 -- The Problem (0:00-0:25)**
Personal story: "I asked ChatGPT for a guitar plan. It was perfect. Then it died in my notes." Two pain points: AI plans don't become calendar routines, and when life interrupts, streaks punish you into quitting.

**Act 2 -- The Solution (0:25-1:50)**
Live walkthrough of the app: plan generation, dashboard, voice briefing, completion, Life Happened, salvage.

**Act 3 -- The Payoff (1:50-2:10)**
Plans page, long-term tracking, monthly extension. Closing line.

## Key Demo Moments (rehearse these)

1. **The story opening** -- record this as a standalone audio take, it sets the emotional tone
2. **The AI plan appearing** -- pause briefly to let the audience read the plan title and month themes
3. **The voice briefing** -- make sure speakers/volume are on, let 3-4 seconds of audio play
4. **The Life Happened bottom sheet** -- this is the emotional core, show it sliding up smoothly
5. **The score animation** -- audiences love watching numbers go up
6. **The closing line** -- "You never start over from zero" -- deliver it clean
